Why is my Email Address Required

This site uses Email to send book requests, notices, and lost email links to the users. If you use an invalid email address then the site will be unable to contact you when someone requests a book, or if you have trouble logging-in. We do not sell our members list to anybody and you will never be spammed as a result of signing up for this service.

My Book Has No ISBN

If the product you are entering does not have an ISBN number, feel free to make one up, but make sure it's something unique so that it doesn't conflict with other products. For instance, a book called Linux Kernel Hacks published in 2001, you might use "LNXKRNLHCKS2001A". Your fake ISBN can contain only numbers and letters, no special characters, dashes, or spaces. And should be no more than 35 characters.
